OUR COMPANY BACKGROUND

Carry and Go is a company established in 2024 and based at UiTM Kedah. The business focuses on producing high-quality bags, offering three main types: backpacks, sling bags, and tote bags. Our aim is to provide stylish and practical bags for students and working professionals who need reliable accessories for their daily activities.

We design our products with a modern and minimalist style, using durable and lightweight materials to ensure comfort and convenience. Each bag is created to help users stay organized while looking fashionable, whether they’re heading to class, the office, or casual outings.

Carry and Go is committed to offering affordable prices without compromising on quality or style. We hope to become a trusted brand, continuously growing and introducing new designs that match the needs and lifestyles of our customers.
